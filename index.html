Cancer Cachexia: More Than Skeletal Muscle Wasting

2022年10月08日
文献笔记
837 words 2 min read

summary

This review was published in 2018 in Trends in Cancer. This corresponding author is Prof.Mauricio Berriel Diaz.In this review authors reinforeced the idea that cachexia syndrome is not only about skeletal muscle wasting, but a multi-tissue syndrome.

glossary

  • Acute-phase response (APR): a prominent systemic reaction of the organism to local or systemic disturbances in its homeostasis caused by infection, tissue injury, trauma, surgery, neoplastic growth, or immunological disorders. It represents an unspecific and acute inflammatory response comprising the synthesis of acute-phase proteins primarily by hepatocytes, which are released into the circulation.
  • Brown adipose tissue(BAT): possesses a number of specialized features enabling it to function as a thermoregulatory organ by generating heat. Brown adipocytes display a high density of mitochondria that are characterized by the unique presence of UCP1.
  • Uncoupling protein 1 (UCP1): located in the inner mitochondrial membrane where it mediates the translocation of protons into the mitochondrial matrix, thereby bypassing proton re-entry via the ATP synthase. Consequently, the energy of the proton gradient is dissipated as heat. The UCP1mediated proton leak lowers the membrane potential, which increases electron flux through the respiratory chain, establishing a futile cycle able to produce considerable amounts of heat, thus increasing energy expenditure

The multi-tissue syndrome of cancer cachexia:

Adipose tissue

The authors talk about the function about adipose tissue in three ways:

The first idea(also more well-accepted)is that adipose tissue loss often hapen before muscle loss.In cancer cachexia, the adipose tissue will undergo strong remodeling, like lipid depletion, extracellular matrix remodeling and enhanced lipolysis activities. cachexia patients oftern observe increased FFA(free fatty acids) in plasma. Enzymes involved in lipolysis like HSL, ATGL also have higher activities.

In the second part, they present the controversy that whether weight loss in cachexia is directly related to increased energy expenditure, and more specifically, the activities of the BAT.In our body, BAT(brown adpise tissue) is responsible for thermogenesis while WAT is for energy storage.Therefore, it may be natural to think that cancer cachexia patient will have more BAT because of their dramatic “energy” loss. That’s the controversial one actually. On the one hand, brown of beige(brown in white) AT marker like CIDE-A and UCP-1 are indeed observed in cachexia. However, researchers couldn’t get consistent results among different mouse models. And most strikingly, UCP-1 knockout and β-adrenergic blockcage mice still develop cachexia. Therefore, the exact answer remains to be explored. The authors propose that alternative futile energy cycles(for example, the creatine kinase cycle) whichi is UCP-1 independent may play a more importat role in this process.

The third part they talks about the systemic effects of AT dysfunction. For instances, poor lipid storage may lead to lipotoxicity and change the levels of adipolines, and lipokines.

Heart

cachexia is also strongly related to heart muscle wasting.Note that some heart illness such as chronic hearta failure can also cause cachexia(specifically referred to as cardiac cachexia).The heart funciton is impaired: reduced fractional shortening(reduced heart rate, impaired cardiac contractility).

cardiac cacheixa also show fibrosis and readopts a fetal gene expression program. But whether the autophagy or proteasome degradation play a more important role remains future validation(C-26 model).

Blood

Kalantar-Zadeh et al. have proposed that thrombocytosis（血小板生成）, i.e., increased platelet number and activation status, as a plausible link between energy wasting and death in chronic disease.

Besides the MDSCs may also play a role in cachexia by associating with APR(acute-phase response)

liver

liver is an important metabolic organ. . Researches have shown the lactate produced by the tumor cells can participate int the lactate cycle and this cause a huge energy loss in cancer condition.

Besides, cachexia mice show reduced ketogenic potential and decreased levels of PPARα in the liver., which leads to increased glucocorticoid levels. PPARα agonist could prevent muscle wasting in tumor-bearing mice.

decreased TG mobilization and hepatic steatosis was also observed in differnet murine cancer cachexia models. And this would contribute to insulin resistance but the data is quite limited.

Also, the cachexia is related to hepatic APR activation for pancreatic or colon cancer. But the direct evidence is lacking.

Other tisses

  • Brain: decreased food intake, altered neuronal output?
  • gut: gut microiota change, gut barrier dysfuntion.
  • pancreas: diabete mellitus(pancreatic cancer cells may secrete exosomes containing adrenomedulie to intefere insulin signaling). (endocrine/exocrine function may be changed).
  • bone: bone loss, increased TGF-β may play a role in that in bone metastasis model.

Concluding

There are two layers of variables of regulation in cancer cachexia: the various tissue and various degress of metabolic dysregulation. The cancer studies should transit to a more systemic level other than just focusing on the local environment.

New idea for me(personally)

  1. In addition to impairing the life quality of cancer patients.The related syndrome also poses a challenge to cancer therapy. Because it have toxicity and can decrease the efficacy of chemotherapy.

Title: Pan-cancer analyses reveal cancer-type-specific fungal ecologies and bacteriome interactions

摘要

这篇文章对35种癌症种的真菌进行了分析，揭示了它们的分布特征、与免疫细胞的联系系以及与肿瘤菌群之间的互作关系。 (This article is more experimental than the other one😊)

研究结果

Sample Info

  1. 17401 patient tissue, blood and plasma across 35 cancer types in four independent cohorts.
  • 其中1个cohort和去年分析最早characterize细菌的文章是同一个。同样引入了paraffin-only和DNA-extraction negative control作为decontamination的参考
  • 另外一个cohort来自TCGA数据库。

真菌定量

作者针对真菌的5.8S核糖体序列，进行荧光定量qCPR。结果发现Breast和Bone中的真菌含量更多。

Self-assessment

在TCGA分析中，细菌的reads数目显著高于真菌reads。

Note: The advantage and disadvantage of WIS and TCGA cohorts:

  • WIS: 一系列无菌操作，对组织进行研磨能够最大程度释放组织中的微生物。并且能够引入空白对照，能够进行成像观察。但是和细菌的16S测序类似，真菌特异性的ITS2 amplicon测序只能够只能测到属，taxonomic resolution。
  • TCGA：宏基因组测序，几乎能够测到所有的真菌基因组。但是只能进行in silico decontamination。

没有一种方法是完美，作者最终综合两个cohort的结果overlap后来推断真菌的数目。

真菌定性观察

作者一共进行了4种染色方法来观察真菌。

  • a fungal cell wall-specific anti-b-glucan antibody with a high false-negative rate.
  • an anti-Aspergillus antibody that also binds several additional fungal species.
  • fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) against three conserved fungal 28S rRNA sequences with selective sensitivity for yeast over hyphal morphologies due to lower hyphae probe penetration.
  • fungal cell wall-specific Gomori methenamine silver (GMS) stain

Intratumoral mycobiome-bacteriome-immunome interactions

Breast cancer is most prevalant co-occurrence spot between fungal and bacteria.

Clinical utility of cancer mycobiome

Just some examples.

  • associate the fungal with disease phenotype, patient survival, and treatment response.

局限性

  1. The cohort variation exists.
  2. The fungal identified may be incomplete. In TCGA analysis, the fungal shows low per-sample genome coverage(undersampling?falsely assignments?)
  3. low biomass, so false-positive results exist. There are no match normal tissues. Inperferect staining methods with varied sensitivity.
  4. couldn’t explain the causility between cancer progression and fungal.

Take home ideas

  1. Even if previous researchers have shown that fungi may play a role in cancer microenvironment, this resaerch shows that systematically(specifically, the pattern), they win.
  2. Decontamination can be done either experimentally or in silico, as long as it’s a robust method. Again, all roads lead to Rome.
  3. http://cancermycobiome.ucsd.edu/
