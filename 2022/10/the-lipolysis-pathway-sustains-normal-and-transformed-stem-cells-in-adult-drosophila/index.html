摘要

Cancer stem cells (CSCs) may be responsible for tumour dormancy, relapse and the eventual death of most cancer patients1. In addition, these cells are usually resistant to cytotoxic conditions.

  • 科学问题:However, very little is known about the biology behind this resistance to therapeutics.
  • 结果：
  1. 敲低COP1(coat protein complex I)-Arf79F(also known as Arf1) can selective killed normal and transformed stem cells through necrosis, but attenuating(make it weaker) the lipolysis pathway, but the differentiated cells are less affected.
  2. The dying stem cells were engulfed by neighbouring differentiated cells through a draper-myoblast city-Racl-basket(JNK)-dependent autophagy pathway.
  3. Arf1 inhibitors can reduce Cancer stem cells in human cancer cell lines.
  • 结论：Thus, normal or cancer stem cells may rely primarily on lipid reserves for energy, in such a way that blocking lipolysis starves them to death. This finding may lead to new therapies that could help to eliminate CSCs in human cancers.

Results

Kill the stem cells

作者想要知道为什么有的癌症干细胞能够逃避杀伤，在这里它们选用果蝇肠道干细胞为模式生物。通过过表达rpr and p53，来试图杀死细胞（包括ISCs, renal and nephric（肾) stem cells。 但是它们的结果显示这只能杀死分化的细胞，而不影响干细胞。 由于之前已经有文献报道JNK信号通路能够引起干细胞过度增殖（但不影响细胞极性），这里作者采用过表达Upd和rpr的方式来试图诱导干细胞凋亡。结果发现能够明显在4天之内使干细胞凋亡。Consistent with this result, expressing a gain-of-function Raf mutant (Rafgof) also accelerated apoptotic cell death of hyperplastic ISCs. 。 此外，它们还发现，如果过表达RasV12，或者激活Notch信号通路的同时，细胞会呈现neoplastic的特性，过度增殖，并且丧失了极性和分化能力。有意思的是，这些细胞不能完全通过过表达rpr清除。而如果敲低Arf79F，则能够显著清除这些干细胞。 这些结果似乎说明，当细胞过度增殖时，它们更容易被清除，干细胞的清除依赖于Arf79F，并且可能与细胞极性是否完整有关。 总之，这一部分的样本信息包括：

  • esg ts>rpr
  • esg ts>RasV12, esg ts>RasV12 rpr, esg ts>RasV12, Arf79F RNAi
  • esg ts>Notch_DN, esg ts> Notch_DN rpr, esg ts> Notch_DN Arf79F RNAi

The function of COP1

In our previous screen, we found that knockdown of COPI components (including Arf79F, the Drosophila homologue of ADP-ribosylation factor 1 (Arf1)) rather than COPII components16 resulted in stem-cell death, suggesting that lipid-droplet usage (lipolysis) rather than the general trafficking machinery between the endoplasmic reticulum and Golgi is important for stem-cell survival. 为了探究Arf1在干细胞清除过程中是否有作用，它们利用esgts,Wgts > X系统来敲低Arf1。结果发现一周之内，大部分的干细胞都被杀伤了，但是类似的方式却不能杀死EC细胞 、马氏管中的stellate细胞（星状细胞）。如果在过表达Ras或者激活Notch信号通路的条件下，也能够通过敲低Arf79F清除transformed stem cells。通过过表达与脂肪酸氧化相关的酶进行回复实验，不过只有过表达Hnf4能够进行rescue。 作者还尝试了敲低一系列和lipolysis有关的基因，观察干细胞的清除情况。 。结果发现都能够促进干细胞的清除。 ACSL, bgm(a very long-chain fatty acid-coA ligase)。

接着，作者利用一个lipolysis reporter，hsp70-GAL4-dHFN4；UAS-nlacZ。结果发现当没有heat shock的条件下,reporter只在ISC和RNSC中表达。而敲低δ-COPRNAi能够完全抑制lipolysis。说明COPI complex的确调控了干细胞中的lipolysi过程。 此外，使用Arf1 inhibitor(brefeldin A, golgicide A, secin H3, LM11 and LG8)，脂肪酸氧化inhibitor((triacsin C, mildronate, etomoxir and enoximone)都能够显著抑制stem cell tumor，但是却不影响正常的干细胞。 总之，这一部分的样本信息包括：

  • esg ts wg ts>Arf79F RNAi（ or OE bmm, Hnf4, scu)
  • To investigat the type of cell death, theye used genotypes that are p35(exclude apoptosis), Atg12RNAi(exclude autophagy).

MARCM：UAS–Hnf4; FRT82B–γCop10, UAS–Scu; FRT82B–γCop10,UAS–Cat; FRT82B–γCop10（I don’t understand why they conduct this experiment?).

How the cell dies

由于前面已经排除了细胞凋亡和自噬，因此它们猜想是肠道干细胞是通过细胞坏死而死亡的。坏死的特征有以下几种：

  • plasma membrane rupture（共聚焦显微镜观察）.
  • PI positive signal(PI染色).
  • ROS accumulation(DHE) and incracellular acidification(Lysotracker). 四种特征分别用了对应的方法去检测。主要的基因型均为esg ts> Notch_DN Arf79F RNAi, esg ts wg ts>Arf79F RNAi.

当然，它们还进行了回复实验，证明干细胞的死亡是依赖于ROS：1)过表达歧化酶Catalase，人为降低ROS含量；2)使用ROS抑制剂，抑制ROS的生成。

Enterocyte可能吞噬了dying stem cells

由于它们发现GFP的信号位于相邻的EC细胞，它们猜想这些dying stem cell是被EC细胞吞噬掉的。由于JNK信号通路参与了细胞死亡的吞噬过程，因此它们想要研究是否JNK信号通路参与了该过程。结果发现

  • ISC中JNK信号通路激活，相邻的EC细胞发生自噬。
  • 激活EC细胞中的JNK信号通路能够引起肠道干细胞死亡。
  • JNK抑制剂处理后能够回复表型。 这些说明JNK信号通路可能参与该过程，但不能说明这是由于EC细胞具有吞噬作用。

CSC cell line

最后它们在人的癌细胞系中进行了一些探索，结果发现在人癌细胞系中使用Arf1抑制剂(brefeldin A and golgicide A)和FAO抑制剂(triascin C and etomoxia)能够显著抑制肿瘤的生长、癌细胞成球率降低。 ![](images/Screenshot 2022-10-11 213839.png)

knowledge

  • The intestinal stem cells (ISCs), located in the posterior midgut, divide once every 24h. the renal and nephric stem cells (RNSCs), located in the Malpighian tubules, divide about once a week4; and the quiescent hindgut intestinal stem cells (HISCs), found at the midgut–hindgut junction, divide only during stressinduced tissue repair5,6. GaSCs are gastric stem cells at the foregut–midgut junction47. GSSCs are gastric stem cells in the middle of the midgut48。
  • Vesicle-mediated COPI and COPII are essential components of the trafficking machinery for vesicle transportation between the endoplasmic reticulum and the Golgi14. In addition, the COPI complex regulates the transport of lipolysis enzymes to the surface of lipid droplets for lipid droplet usage15 (Extended Data Fig. 2a).
